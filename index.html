I am currently on the second hardware revision (the old one broke haha). PLA is not the strongest material and its major downfall is its tendency to melt in a car on a hot day. Revision 2 was a challenge to myself to make all the motors and electronics contained so as to look more like a commercial product.

The software is really cool. It’s entirely controlled by a web browser. The brain is a Raspberry Pi and hosts a WiFi hotspot so that phones can connect and control.
